## Why are women under-represented in politics?
- British politics can be **male dominated**, aggressive and have **sexist attitudes** towards women MPs
- **Harassment and discrimination** towards female politicians
- Women are on average less likely to be selected as candidates
- Voters mat be less likely to **vote** for a female candidate due to out-dated and sexist attitudes
