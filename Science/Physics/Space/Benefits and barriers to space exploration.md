### Other benefits

#### Economic growth
Large amounts of investment by the government in specialist industries can help create technology and industries that would not have developed without large amounts of government investment and buying things without the need for it to be economically viable.

#### Climate and weather monitoring 
Space based imaging can help to stop natural disasters, monitor the effects of climate change and help predict and detect severe weather. This has a large impact on a society, far outweighing the cost of the space program.

#### Scientific discovery's made in space
The science that can be done with a space program is deeply beneficial to the culture and academic sector of a country.
