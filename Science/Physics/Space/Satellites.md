
## Types and uses for Satellites

- Geostationary
	- Orbital period of **24 hours** so it stays in the **same place** relative to the position of the earth.
	- Traditional Satellite internet and TV
	- Allows a ground antenna to be pointed at the same place all the time
- LEO
	- Low Earth Orbit
	- Contains things that need **Low Latency** such as networking constellations
	- This is where the ISS is 
	- Where imaging satellites are 
- MEO
	- Medium earth orbit
	- Contains GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS and Galileo
	- Some specialised communication satellites
- Polar
	- Orbit "Up and Down" the earth passing over the poles 
	- Usually in LEO 
	- Used for things that need to cover the whole earth e.g. imaging
