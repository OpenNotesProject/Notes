A wave has 4 measurements:
### Amplitude 
The amplitude of a wave is **half the length from the peak to the trough** or **the distance from the peak of the wave from the centre axis**.

### Wavelength
The wavelength of a wave is the length of one wave.

### Period
The period of a wave is the time taken for one wave to pass.

### Frequency
The frequency of a wave is the number of waves that pass in a set amount of time
