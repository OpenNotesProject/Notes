> Any substance that **returns lost essential elements to the soil**

All Fertiliser are:
- Soluble in water
- Contains high [[% Compisition]] of 
	- K(Potassium)
	- N(Nitrogen)
	- P(Phosphorous)


> [!NOTE] Manufacturing
> The fertilisers ammonia and nitric acid can be made using the [[The Haber Process]] and the [[The Ostwald Process]] respectively
